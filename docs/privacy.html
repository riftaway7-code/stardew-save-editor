Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Stardew Valley Save Editor ("the Site", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. By using the Site, you agree to this policy.

Your save files

The Stardew Valley Save Editor runs entirely in your web browser. When you load a save file, that file is read and edited locally on your own device. Your save file is never uploaded to us or to any server, and we never see, store, or transmit its contents. When you finish editing, the edited file is downloaded directly back to your device.

Information we collect

The Site itself does not ask you for or collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or account details. We do not run our own analytics or tracking on your save data.

Children's privacy

This Site is a general-audience tool and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.
